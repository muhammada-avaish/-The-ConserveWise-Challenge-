Tagline: “One app. One planet. One mission — ConserveWise.”

Cast:

  • Ameirah – The Advert Challenger (doubtful at first)
  • Bryan – The Presenter
  • Ahmad – The Tech Expert
  • Jensen – The Nature Advocate

Scene 1 – The Pitch

(Bryan, Ahmad, and Jensen stand in front of a colorful “ConserveWise” banner. Ameirah walks in, looking skeptical.)

Ameirah (folding arms):
 Oh great, another “Save the Earth” app. Like that’s going to change anything.

Bryan (confidently):
 Actually, it might! Meet ConserveWise — an app that helps everyone save our planet, one daily goal at a time.

Scene 2 – The Challenge Begins

Ameirah (teasing):
 Daily goals? Like what — “Hug a tree and feel better”?

Ahmad (laughing, holding phone):
 Not exactly!
Each day, ConserveWise gives a real conservation goal.
 Like today — October 26 — the goal is:
🪠 “Conserve water by fixing leaky faucets.”

Jensen:
 Tomorrow’s goal might be “Turn off lights when you leave a room,” or “Carry a reusable bottle.”
Simple actions that add up to big change!

Ameirah (curious):
 So it’s not about earning points — it’s about learning and doing?

Bryan:
 Exactly! ConserveWise guides people to live sustainably — one step, one day, one goal at a time.

Scene 3 – The Awareness Message

Ahmad:
 Every goal teaches us something about saving water, energy, or reducing waste.
It even explains why it matters — so we understand the impact behind our actions.

Jensen (passionately):
 Because when everyone takes small steps — fixing taps, switching off fans, avoiding plastic — the planet feels the difference!

Ameirah (starting to smile):
 So… ConserveWise isn’t just another app — it’s like a daily reminder to care.

Bryan (nodding):
 Exactly! You can check it out at https://conservewise.glide.page/dl/77a54a and start your conservation journey today.

Scene 4 – The Challenger Turns Supporter

(Ameirah steps forward, smiling confidently.)

Ameirah:
 Alright, you win. I challenged your ad — but you proved your point.
ConserveWise really makes conservation a daily habit!

All together (raising their phones or hands):
 🌿 Be aware. Take action. Conserve — with ConserveWise! 🌎
